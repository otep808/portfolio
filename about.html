A second-year BSIT student with a love for building things — whether that's a web app, a Python script, or a freshly improvised gadget.

Joseph "Otep" Callos is a student at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela who thrives on blending technology with creativity. He builds web systems that are clean and scalable, designs interfaces that feel intuitive, and solves problems with persistence and systematic thinking.

Whether it's writing Python automation, structuring HTML/CSS for maintainable design, organizing group presentations into polished stories, or troubleshooting routers — his work is always guided by functionality and imagination. He's motivated by his family and friends, and believes that every second counts.

Beyond academics, Otep plays guitar, practices sewing, competes in badminton, and collects building blocks. He handles stress by listening to Rock, Country, and Folk music — his favorites being The Beatles and Jim Croce.
